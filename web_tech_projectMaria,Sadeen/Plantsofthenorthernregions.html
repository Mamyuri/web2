Plant of Nourthern Region

Olives

Growth sites of Olives in Saudi Arabia:Olive trees grow well in warm and moderate regions in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. Olive trees can be found in the northern regions of Saudi Arabia, such as Al-Jouf and Hail

Care for Olives :

-Irrigation: : Olive trees require moderate watering during their growth and fruit production period.

-Fertilization: : Organic or chemical fertilizers can be added at specific times during the year to improve tree growth and fruit quality.

-Pruning: Prune dry leaves and old branches from the tree to improve tree ventilation and fruiting.
