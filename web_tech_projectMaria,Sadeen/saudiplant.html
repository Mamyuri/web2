Saudi Common Plants

Palm Tree

Growth sites of Palm Tree in Saudi Arabia: The palm tree mainly grows throughout the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, but it is extensively cultivated in dry and desert regions such as Qassim, Riyadh, Al-Ahsa, and Al-Madinah.

Care for Palm Tree:

-Irrigation: Palm trees should be regularly watered during the summer season and the soil should not be left dry.

-Fertilization: Palm trees should be regularly watered during the summer season and the soil should not be left dry.

-Pruning: Palm trees should be regularly watered during the summer season and the soil should not be left dry.

-Insects and pests: Monitor the palm tree regularly and take necessary preventive measures to control pests and diseases that may affect it.

Uses: The palm tree is considered an important tree in desert agriculture, producing delicious and nutritious dates. It is also used in traditional agriculture and for landscaping gardens and green spaces.
