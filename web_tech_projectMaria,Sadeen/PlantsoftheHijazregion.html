Plant of Hijaz Region

Hijazi Clover

Growth sites of Hijazi Clover in Saudi Arabia: Hijazi Clover can grow in several regions in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, such as Al-Hijaz, Al-Qassim, Hail, and Riyadh.

Care for Hijazi Clover:

-Irrigation: Hijazi Clover needs regular watering during its growth period, especially during dry spells.

-Fertilization: Providing appropriate organic and chemical fertilizers is preferred to enhance plant growth and flowering.

-Pruning: Hijazi Clover may require regular pruning to remove dead or damaged leaves.

-Insects and pests: Make sure to monitor the plant regularly to check for insects or pests and take necessary treatment measures.
