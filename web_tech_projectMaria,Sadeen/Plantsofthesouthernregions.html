Plant of Southern Region

Pomegranate

Growth sites of Pomegranate in Saudi Arabia: The pomegranate tree grows well in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia in several regions, including Najran and Jazan.

Care for Pomegranate:

-Irrigation: It is important to water the pomegranate tree regularly during the summer season, especially when the fruits are in the growth stage.

-Fertilization:Organic fertilizers or special fertilizers for fruit-bearing trees can be added regularly to enhance tree growth and increase fruit production

-Pruning: The pomegranate tree may require regular pruning to remove old or damaged branches and encourage tree fruiting.

-Insects and pests:Pomegranate trees need proper care through pest and common disease control that may affect them.
